Advice

Keep in Mind
  • Remember to always report when scammed. Less than 14 percent of scam victims report such instances.
  • Reporting the scam will help authorities track down the culprits and prevent others from falling prey.

    • How do scammers acquire the actual money?
  • NEVER pay upfront for promises such as, mortgage relief, a job, loan offers, or lotteries.
  • Hang up automated robotic calls. These pre-recorded are illegal and most likely fraudulent.
  • Be skeptical. Never believe the caller ID. Some telecommunication firms provide an automatic notification if a specific caller is presumed to be a scam, based on reports from other users.Source
